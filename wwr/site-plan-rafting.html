WhiteWater Rafting Site Plan

By Damian Fink

Overview

This document serves as the foundational blueprint for the WhiteWater Rafting website, detailing the company's online strategy, target audience, branding guidelines, design approach, and wireframes.

Purpose

The purpose of the WhiteWater Rafting website is to provide information about our rafting services, showcase the beauty and excitement of the outdoor rafting experience, and enable customers to book trips online.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure seekers of all ages, families looking for engaging vacations, and outdoor enthusiasts interested in exploring the rivers of Utah.

Scenario Questions

Q: Can families with children participate in rafting trips?
A: Absolutely! We have various trips suitable for all ages.

Q: What kind of preparations are needed for a rafting trip?
A: Guests should be prepared with sun protection, water shoes, and a sense of adventure. We provide the rest!

Q: Are there any discounts for large groups or repeat customers?
A: Yes, we offer discounts for both large groups and our returning adventurers.

Typography

Headings Example Font

The font used for headings is 'Lato', a sans-serif font that is clean and modern.

Normal Text/Paragraph Example Font

The font for normal text is 'Roboto', also sans-serif, known for readability and friendly appearance.

Colored Callout Example Font

For special callouts and accent text, we use 'Roboto' in our accent colors to highlight important information.
